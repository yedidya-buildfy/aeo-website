Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

Introduction

AEO Blog Generator ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") provides automated blog generation and SEO optimization for Shopify stores. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle store and user data when you install or use our App.

Information We Collect

When you install the App, we collect data directly from Shopify's APIs, including:

  • Store domain, owner name, and email
  • Access tokens required for API communication
  • Store content (product info, pages, and blog data) only as needed to generate and publish content
  • Usage analytics such as generation count and last activity date
  • Subscription details (plan, billing status, Shopify charge ID)

We do not collect customer or order data from your store unless explicitly required for a feature.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Generate and publish SEO-optimized blog content
  • Analyze keywords and site structure to improve relevance
  • Manage app functionality, billing, and authentication
  • Provide customer support and technical updates

We do not sell or share your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

  • All communication between your store and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data is securely stored in a PostgreSQL database with restricted access
  • When you uninstall the App, your data is deleted within 30 days
  • Backups (if created) are automatically removed after 90 days

Third-Party Services

We rely on:

  • Shopify API – to access store data and publish content
  • Google Gemini AI – to generate blog text and analyze keywords
  • Hosting Provider (Render) – to store authentication and app data securely

Each service complies with its own privacy policy: Shopify Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access or delete your data anytime by uninstalling the App
  • Request data deletion or export via email: yedidyadan33@gmail.com
  • Disable automation features from the App settings
